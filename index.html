    FunFirst

    Funfirst started as a contract manufacturing outfit in 2001, focused on Consumer Electronics & Home Appliance products. It went on to become one of the largest electronic contract manufacturing company in India.

    Lal10

    "We are creating a maker’s revolution by digitizing micro and small manufacturers to take their creative products to global markets. India has over 1.2mn such MSMEs involved in the manufacturing of apparel, home furnishings, decor products. We have digitized 30k SKUs to date and are the largest vertical platform for MSMEs for cross-border wholesale in India."

    StanPlus

    "At StanPlus, we care for you & are constantly working towards making a better environment for those in need. Our global experience in medical transportation services allows us to deliver a perfect, reliable & glitch-free service to all."

    Prescinto

    "Prescinto has a unique combination of in-depth sector specific tools to identify reasons of underperformance, have them visualized at a portfolio, plant and a device level and then convert them into work orders for site teams. Prescinto was also able to give us the customization in dashboards and reporting that we follow as a best practice across our group."

    WYO

    "Wear Your Opinion was founded with nothing but a just a small room full of t-shirts. Over all these years, we have in baby steps, grown from rooms to warehouses to physical stores in different parts of the country. Although a lot of things have changed, yet a lot has remained the same as well, for instance, the satisfaction that we could bring to our first customer till the very recent one. "

    About

    As operating investors, we wish to move beyond capital infusion, and create an ecosystem, which helps our companies succeed in building sustainable operating cash flow models, and thrives on entrepreneurial passion.

    ​

    While we are focused on generating financial gains, we wish to leave a larger footprint around the ecosystem we develop. Our companies will be focused on generating a positive socio-economic impact, while operating as "responsible businesses".
